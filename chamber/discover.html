Demographics

Port Harcourt is the Capital and largest city in Rivers State and the fifth most populous city after Lagos, Kano, Ibadan, and Benin. It was founded in 1912 and named after lewis Vernon Harcourt.

  • Population: Approximately 3,480,000 residents.
  • Languages Spoken: English, Pigin, Ogoni, Ikwere, and Igbo languages.
  • Residential Areas: Mix of luxury apartments, family villas, and studios.
