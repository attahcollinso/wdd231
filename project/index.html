Project Site Plan.

1. Site name

Attahco Stores: This name Attah is from the igala people which means head or leader. Attahco Stores is comming to be a disciple leader in the online space.

2. Site purpose:

The purpose of this e-commerce site is to provide online products and services that people that visit the site can order for and have it delivered to their door steps. It will also aim at gaining the trust of people to online transactions.

3. Scenarios:

These are anticipated questions from the visitors of this site. Some of the scenarios include; 1. What services do you offer? 2. Do you also have a physical store where someone can visit?

4. Color Schema:

I will make use of four color schmes. They include blue, beige, and white. The blue will be used at the heading and footer. The beige will be used as the body background color. The white color will be used at the navigation text and paragraph text.

5. Typography:

I will make us of "Roboto:wght@100;400;500,bold" font weight throughtout the site.
